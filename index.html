Library

Library

Well planned school library service inspires students to maximum use of it and create in them a liking for intellectual pursuits. We deem it a great responsibility in equipping the library with adequate space, suitable furniture, affluence of useful & standard books of world Literature, well known story books, biographies, encyclopedias, books with moral anecdotes, civic and social sense illustrated books apart from subject reference books, standard magazines, journals and leading newspapers. the librarian consider the interest of the students and keeps them well informed of the new arrivals. Ample time is provided for the students in the school time table for study in the library as well as for borrowing books.
